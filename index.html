🔮 About Oracle Sandi Labb

Oracle Sandi Labb is a mystical digital sanctuary where ancient tarot wisdom meets modern intuitive guidance. We believe the cosmos speaks to all of us — sometimes we just need a mirror to hear it more clearly.

Our oracle is designed to help you reflect deeply, ask honest questions, and find grounded answers in the symbolism of the Major Arcana and the patterns of your birth chart.

What We Offer
🌠
Birth Chart Reading
A real astronomical calculation of your Sun, Moon, and Ascendant based on your birth data — giving you a precise cosmic profile with sky map and planetary positions.
📅
Daily Horoscope
Today's personalised guidance for your sun sign — including energy levels for love, career, and luck, plus lucky numbers and colours.
💞
Compatibility Checker
Discover the cosmic bond between two signs with a full compatibility score across emotional, communication, passion, and spiritual dimensions.
🃏
Tarot Oracle
Draw from the full Major Arcana — with real Rider-Waite card art — across Yes/No, Love, Career, and General spreads. Each reading is unique and personal.
✨
AI Oracle Chat
A conversational oracle powered by Claude AI that draws a tarot card with every reply, interprets it in the context of your question, and offers compassionate, grounded guidance.

We don't predict your fate — we illuminate the possibilities. Tarot is a tool for reflection, not prophecy. The power to shape your path has always been yours.
